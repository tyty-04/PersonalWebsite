Fateweaver

The idea of fateweaver was to combine the in depth build crafting of MMORPGs with the fast paced gameplay of ARPGs. We wanted to create a game where players could experience the leveling and endgame of something like World of Warcraft in the roughly hour long runs of a rougelike.
